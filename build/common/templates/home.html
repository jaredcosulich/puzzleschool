For Those Who Crave Challenge

The Puzzle School is focused on encouraging a specific process:

The process of figuring out (puzzling out) a challenge, of developing and testing hypotheses, learning from failures and successes, iterating and persisting closer and closer toward a solution.

Our educational puzzles are built to be fun and engaging purely because they present challenges that you can figure out.

There are no high scores, just a sense of accomplishment and progress as you figure out increasingly complex challenges.
