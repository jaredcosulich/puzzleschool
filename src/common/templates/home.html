Learning That Is Fun Because It's Challenging

We believe that all people love to learn when that learning is meaningful and appropriately challenging.

The Puzzle School is focused on encouraging a specific process around such learning:

The process of figuring out (puzzling out) a challenge, of developing and testing hypotheses, learning from failures and successes, iterating and persisting closer and closer toward a goal.

Our educational puzzles are built to be fun and engaging purely because they present challenges that you can figure out.

There are no high scores, just a sense of accomplishment and progress as you figure out increasingly complex challenges.

We hope to some day create physical spaces or even a school where students of all ages will truly enjoy being challenged.
